  • What is the difference between UI and UX?

    Answer : UX- user experience designer course encompasses a user's entire interaction with a product or website. In contrast, the User interface (UI) in digital design refers to the interactivity, appearance, and feel of a product screen or online page. Developing a memorable user experience and an engaging user interface is imperative for a business to succeed online.
